# VelaMap

**AI Knowledge Operating System**

Structure, map, and navigate knowledge in the age of AI.

---

## 🧠 What is VelaMap?

VelaMap is an AI-powered system for transforming fragmented information into structured, connected, and navigable knowledge.

It helps individuals and teams build their own **knowledge maps** — enabling better understanding, exploration, and reasoning over complex systems.

---

## ✨ Core Capabilities

- 🗺️ **Knowledge Mapping**  
  Convert documents, notes, and data into structured knowledge graphs

- 🔗 **Semantic Linking**  
  Automatically connect related concepts across different sources

- 🧭 **Path & Navigation**  
  Explore knowledge through paths, not just search

- 🤖 **AI-Native Query**  
  Ask questions and reason over your knowledge in natural language

- 📊 **Graph Interface**  
  Visualize and interact with knowledge as a dynamic system

---

## 🚀 Positioning

VelaMap is not just a tool — it is an **AI Knowledge OS**.

A system layer for organizing, understanding, and operating on knowledge.

---

## 🧩 Principles

> Knowledge is not documents.  
> Knowledge is structure.

- Systems over documents  
- Context over prompts  
- Graphs over linear text  
- Exploration over retrieval  
- Structure enables reasoning
